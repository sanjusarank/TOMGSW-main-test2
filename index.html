Our Story

We are manufacturers of pet bottle goli soda in Chennai. We are also distributing our product across Tamilnadu either directly or through distributors. Goli Soda, a popular Indian term for carbonated beverages sold in pet bottles and glass bottles, has a nostalgic appeal that resonates with people of all ages. The Goli Soda business thrives on the timeless charm and growing demand for traditional carbonated beverages. With an increasing trend towards natural and artisanal products, Goli Soda stands out as a refreshing alternative to mainstream sodas. Additionally, the appeal of Goli Soda extends to events, parties, and restaurants, making it a versatile product for various occasions.
